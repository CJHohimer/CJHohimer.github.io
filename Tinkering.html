Cars

I love to tinker with all kinds of different projects. I don't always take as many photos along the way as I should but here are a few of the things I enjoy working on. I own several cars I enjoy restoring. Each in a different state of unfinished. My wife loves to take photos of me working on various projects, often when I am not aware she is doing so. Most recently I finished rebuilding the engine on my '68 MG Midget.

My capstone project was to design and build a fuel cell car for the Shell Eco Marathon. We unfortunately did not get to complete but we still finished the build in time to graduate. We used the front end from a recumbent bike with a custom built rack and pinion steering mechanism. We used an OEM electric hub motor in the rear of the three wheeled car for propulsion.

Trains

I am a train enthusiast. My grandfather built several large scale trains and as a kid I would ride them around his yard on a track he had set up. I look forward to someday installing them around my own yard. They are fun to maintain and tinker with.
